Jobs COVID-19


Did you lose your job during this pandemic?

Here is a tiny little form that will help you to get discovered by potential hiring organizations and good friends who can connect you to them.

How does this work?
  • Fill up the form with your details.
  • The entries of this form will be floated to a publicly accessible database through this website.
  • We are reaching out to recruiters, HR teams and organizations that are currently hiring and looking for people like YOU.
What we don't do.
  • We do not sell your data or spam you.
  • We are not promising any jobs for the people registering their information.
We are a group of independent volunteers trying to help people get back to workforce as early as possible. No gimmicks. No cross-selling. Just a simple volunteer effort.
